Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy for agricultural equipment?

Customers can return agricultural equipment within 15 days of purchase, provided the equipment is in its original condition with all accessories and packaging intact.

How can I track my service request?

You can track the status of your equipment service by logging into your AgriMart account and navigating to the "Service Status" section.

Do you provide on-site repair services?

Yes, we provide on-site repair services for agricultural equipment in select regions. Please contact our support team for more details and availability in your area.
